Lessons & Schooling

Robyn is based in Burwash near Heathfield, East Sussex. She gives lessons or schools horses at your own yard or in hired arenas across East Sussex, Kent and West Sussex.

  • Individual sessions: 45 minutes — £45 + travel
  • Schooling without owner present: + £5 extra for tack up & wash down
  • Discounts for locals (within 5 miles) or multiple bookings at same yard

Monthly visits to Surrey/Hampshire (Leatherhead, Guildford, Elstead, Liphook, Midhurst, etc.) offer 45-minute sessions for £55, all-inclusive. Other areas and clinics also available on request.

Robyn is a fully accredited Pony Club Coach (Romney Marsh Pony Club) and provides regular flatwork training in Sandhurst, East Sussex with discounts for RMPC members.

Wireless training system available for larger arenas or hearing support—use your own jack-plug earphones or the provided set.

Please call or email to discuss your requirements.
